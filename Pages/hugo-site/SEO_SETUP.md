## üéØ Google AdSense Setup

### Step 1: Get Your AdSense Account
1. Visit [Google AdSense](https://www.google.com/adsense/)
2. Sign up with your Google account
3. Add your site URL: `https://daily2translate.github.io/workspace-domain-repo/`
4. Wait for approval (usually 1-2 weeks)

### Step 2: Get Your Publisher ID
Once approved, find your publisher ID in AdSense dashboard:
- Format: `ca-pub-XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX`

### Step 3: Add Publisher ID to Hugo Config
Edit `hugo.toml`:

```toml
[params]
  googleAdsense = "ca-pub-XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX"  # Your actual ID
```

### Step 4: Create Ad Units
1. In AdSense dashboard, create ad units
2. Get your ad slot IDs
3. Replace placeholder IDs in ad partials:
   - `layouts/partials/adsense.html` ‚Üí Replace `XXXXXXXXXX`
   - `layouts/partials/adsense-article.html` ‚Üí Replace `YYYYYYYYYY`

### Step 5: Add Ads to Your Site

**In layouts/index.html** (homepage):
```html
{{ partial "adsense.html" . }}
```

**In blog posts** (edit post template or use shortcode):
```html
{{ partial "adsense-article.html" . }}
```

## üìù Content SEO Tips

### For Each Blog Post:
1. **Use descriptive titles** (50-60 characters)
2. **Write meta descriptions** in frontmatter:
   ```yaml
   ---
   title: "Your Post Title"
   description: "A compelling 150-160 character description"
   ---
   ```
3. **Use heading hierarchy** (H1 ‚Üí H2 ‚Üí H3)
4. **Add internal links** to related posts
5. **Use keywords naturally** (don't stuff)
6. **Include images** with alt text
7. **Write quality content** (Google rewards this most)

### Example Post Frontmatter:
```yaml
---
title: "Understanding Consciousness in AI"
date: 2024-02-17
draft: false
description: "Explore the hard problem of consciousness and its implications for artificial intelligence, examining qualia, integrated information theory, and moral questions."
keywords: ["AI consciousness", "philosophy of mind", "qualia", "hard problem"]
tags: ["AI", "Philosophy", "Consciousness"]
categories: ["Philosophy"]
author: "Thoughtful Observer"
---
```
